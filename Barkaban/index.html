NICE TO MEET YOU!

Established in 1964, Barbakan Delicatessen is a nationally award-winning craft bakery and continental delicatessen set in the heart of Chorlton, Manchester.
Our breads, cakes and pastries are baked throughout the night on-site and presented fresh & hot every morning to our beautiful delicatessen - much to the delight of our Chorlton neighbours who can smell the goods throughout the village...

Enjoy a hot ciabatta sizzler, fresh quiche, or homemade soup made to order from our kitchen to enjoy on our outdoor seated terrace...

Peruse our endless varieties of local and continental cheese, olives and charcuterie from our deli counters...
Select a beautiful, bespoke wicker hamper for a thoughtful gift...
Browse for your essentials in our unique shop and be personally served by one of our knowledgeable shop
assistants...

Enjoy a cup of award-winning Italian roasted espresso.. At weekends enjoy our infamous outdoor German sausage pan, complete with roast potatoes, fried onions and chorizo rice...

BAKERY

We specialise in continental loaves, such as our Award-Winning German Norlander, French Campagrain, Italian Contadina, Polish Black. Not forgetting our famous Chorlton Sourdough! We bake over 75 varieties of hand-moulded loaves and produce over 15,000 per week!
